CLEAN ENERGY REGULATOR

A Breath of Fresh Air for the Clean Energy Regulator Website

Navigating the Clean Energy Regulator (CER) website of Australia felt like an intricate maze, with misaligned elements and buried information.

The goal was to breathe new life into the user experience and help users effortlessly navigate the website to apply for various schemes.

Goals and Objectives
The project aimed to transform the application process for schemes like the Clean Emissions Reduction Fund, NGER, and the Renewable Energy Target scheme. Additionally, the team set out to polish the website's user interface, making it shine like the bright future of clean energy.
Target Audience

Businesses eager to join forces with CER initiatives and individuals keen on exploring clean energy policies and programs.

Role and Responsibilities

Working in unison, the team embarked on research and definition phases, unravelling the tangled web of user tests and heuristic evaluations. Then, each member crafted their own dazzling prototypes for desktop and mobile versions, ensuring a responsive and fluid navigation experience.

Process and Methodology

The project followed a comprehensive, creative process, including research, ideation, prototyping, testing, and implementation.

The team conducted user tests, reimagined the navigation bar, and introduced a sleek style guide to elevate the website's design and usability.

Interaction design skills took center stage with techniques like enchanting parallax effects on desktop.

Challenges & Solutions

The team conjured up an innovative "Ask AI Anything" feature, harnessing the power of ChatGPT API for seamless information retrieval. Overcoming the limitations of Figma, the parallax effect was ingeniously implemented using smart animate and multiple pages.

Outcome & Learnings

The revitalized website offered a refreshing and enhanced user experience, illuminating the path to clean energy initiatives. The project showcased the importance of style guides, interactive design, and honed the team's expertise in various design tools. Their creative journey was rewarded with a stellar grade in the class.
